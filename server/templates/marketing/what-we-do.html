What We Do

Kahraba connects you with verified, licensed electricians for all your home electrical needs in Jordan.

Our Services

  1. 1

    Electrical Repairs

    From flickering lights to faulty outlets, our electricians diagnose and fix electrical problems quickly and safely. We handle everything from minor repairs to complex troubleshooting.

  2. 2

    New Installations

    Need new outlets, switches, or lighting fixtures? Our electricians install new electrical components to code, ensuring safety and reliability for your home.

  3. 3

    Circuit Breaker Services

    Tripping breakers or outdated panels? We upgrade, repair, and maintain circuit breakers and electrical panels to keep your home's electrical system running smoothly.

  4. 4

    Wiring & Rewiring

    Whether you're renovating or dealing with old wiring, our electricians provide safe, code-compliant wiring services for your home.

  5. 5

    Emergency Services

    Electrical emergencies don't wait, and neither do we. Get connected with an electrician quickly when you need urgent electrical assistance.
